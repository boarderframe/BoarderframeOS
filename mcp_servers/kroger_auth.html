Step 1: Understand What This Does

This will connect your personal Kroger.com account to the MCP server so you can:

  • Add items to your actual Kroger cart
  • Access your loyalty card information
  • Use delivery services with your account
Security: This uses Kroger's official OAuth2 system. Your password stays with Kroger - we only get permission to access your cart and profile.

Step 2: Start Authentication

Click this button to authorize the MCP server with your Kroger account:

Step 3: What Happens Next

  1. You'll be redirected to Kroger.com to log in
  2. Kroger will ask you to authorize the MCP server
  3. You'll be redirected back here with a success message
  4. Your authentication will be saved for cart operations

Step 4: Test Your Connection

Once authenticated, you can test these endpoints:

POST http://localhost:9004/cart
GET http://localhost:9004/identity/loyalty
PUT http://localhost:9004/cart/items
